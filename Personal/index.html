Welcome to my space in the web!


My name is Andres Mora and I am a Ph.D. in Aerospace Engin eer (Space Robotics). I have worked in various robotic fields including search-and-rescue, teleoperation, path planning, lunar exploration, social mobile robots and human-robot interaction.


Currently I work as a post-doctoral researcher at the Environments Robotics and Instrumentation Laboraroty (EERIL), at the School of Earth and Space Exploration, Arizona State University. Work at the EERIL involves working with the design, implementation and testing of new instrumentation and robotic technologies suitable for extreme environments like subglacial lakes in the Antartica, hot springs in Nevada or hydrothermal vents in the bottom of the ocean. These novel technologies would enable scientists better understand a vast number of phenomena in our planet and in many other celestial bodies.


I am also interested in football, travelling, scuba diving, snowboarding, photography and just recently started to work towards my pilot license.
